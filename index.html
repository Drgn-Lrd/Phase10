      How to use this scorecard

      • Phase column: tap a player's phase number to see what that phase requires.
      • Cleared checkbox: check this at the end of a round if the player laid down their phase.
      • Score to add: type the point value, or tap Calc to count up remaining cards.

      Point values

      • Cards 1–9: 5 points
      • Cards 10–12: 10 points
      • Skips: 15 points
      • Wilds: 25 points
