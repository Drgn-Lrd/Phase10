    The 10 Phases

      How to Use the App

      • Phase Column: Tap any player's Phase number to quickly view what that specific phase requires.
      • Cleared Checkbox: Check this at the end of a round if the player successfully laid down their phase.
      • Score Addition: Enter the point value manually, or tap the Calc button to count up remaining cards.

      Point Values

      • Cards 1-9: 5 Points
      • Cards 10-12: 10 Points
      • Skips: 15 Points
      • Wilds: 25 Points
